Ideogram's Revolutionary Text Rendering

Every AI image generator has struggled with one fundamental problem: rendering readable, accurate text within images. Ideogram achieves near-perfect text rendering by training its model with a focus on typography and graphic design datasets. Ideogram vs DALL·E 3 for Text Rendering

DALL·E 3 made significant improvements to text rendering compared to earlier models, but Ideogram still leads by a significant margin. In head-to-head tests, Ideogram achieves accurate text rendering in approximately 90% of generations, while DALL·E 3 achieves approximately 70%, and Midjourney/Stable Diffusion rarely exceed 30–40% for complex text.

The gap is most apparent for: long strings of text (more than 2–3 words), decorative or script fonts, text with special characters, and layouts with multiple separate text elements. For these cases, Ideogram is the clear choice.
