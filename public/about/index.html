About ImageToPrompt

Making AI image generation accessible to everyone — no expertise required.

What Is ImageToPrompt?

ImageToPrompt is a free, browser-based AI tool that analyzes any image you upload and instantly generates an optimized text prompt for your chosen AI image or video generator. No login. No subscription. No friction.

Whether you're a designer trying to recreate a style, a photographer looking to generate variations, or a complete beginner exploring AI art for the first time — ImageToPrompt gives you a production-ready prompt in seconds.

Why I Built It

When I started experimenting with AI image generators, the hardest part wasn't the tools themselves — it was writing good prompts. Every model has its own syntax, its own quirks, its own vocabulary. Midjourney wants --ar flags and stylize values. Stable Diffusion expects weighted terms and negative prompts. Flux handles natural language differently than DALL-E 3.

I kept wishing I could just point at an image and say "give me the prompt for this." So I built it.

ImageToPrompt started as a personal tool. When I shared it with a few friends who were learning Midjourney, the response was immediate — they found it genuinely useful. That's when I decided to build it properly and make it free for everyone.

Our Mission

AI image generation is one of the most exciting creative technologies in decades. But the learning curve — especially around prompt engineering — keeps a lot of people from getting the most out of these tools. ImageToPrompt exists to close that gap.

The mission is simple: anyone with an image and an idea should be able to generate great AI art, regardless of how much they know about prompt syntax.

What Makes It Different

Most prompt tools are generic. ImageToPrompt is built around the specific syntax and vocabulary of each model:

  • 7 image models — Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, Flux, DALL-E 3, Adobe Firefly, Leonardo AI, and Ideogram — each with model-specific formatting
  • 8 video models — Veo, Kling, Runway, Pika, Luma, Sora, Minimax, and Stable Video — with motion, duration, and camera movement controls
  • No login required — 10 free analyses per day, no account needed
  • Claude AI vision — powered by Anthropic's Claude, which reads images with nuance: lighting, composition, mood, color palette, style
  • Privacy-first — your images are processed in real time and never stored on our servers

The Technology

ImageToPrompt is built with React and TypeScript on the frontend, deployed on Vercel's edge network for fast global response times. Image analysis is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI, which provides the visual understanding needed to generate high-quality, contextually accurate prompts. Rate limiting is handled through Upstash Redis to keep the tool free and fair for everyone.

ImageToPrompt is my most-used personal project, and I'm committed to keeping it free and improving it based on user feedback.
