Ideogram's Revolutionary Text Rendering

Every AI image generator has struggled with one fundamental problem: rendering readable, accurate text within images. Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL·E 3 — all produce garbled, misspelled, or distorted text when asked to include words in generated images. Ideogram was built specifically to solve this problem.

Ideogram achieves near-perfect text rendering by training its model with a focus on typography and graphic design datasets. A prompt like "a vintage concert poster with the text 'THE MIDNIGHT' in bold art deco typography" produces accurately rendered, legible text in a stylistically appropriate font — something no other major model does reliably.

Typography and Design Capabilities

Text in Quotes
Wrap exact text to be rendered in double quotes within your prompt: "a poster saying 'GRAND OPENING'". Ideogram treats quoted strings as literal text to render.
Typography Styles
Specify font personality: bold serif, elegant script, geometric sans-serif, vintage display, handwritten brush. Ideogram maps these to appropriate typefaces in the output.
Layout Description
Describe text placement: "centered headline at the top", "text arranged in a circular pattern", "three-column layout". Ideogram handles complex compositional text layouts.
Design Styles
Specify the design genre: "Art Deco poster", "Swiss International style", "retro 80s neon", "minimalist corporate". Ideogram's training covers a broad range of graphic design history.

Graphic Design Use Cases

Ideogram is the AI generator of choice for graphic designers, brand designers, and marketing professionals who need to generate design concepts quickly. Its unique combination of text rendering and aesthetic quality makes it particularly valuable for:

  • Poster design concepts: Event posters, movie posters, music posters with accurate typography and atmospheric illustration
  • Logo and badge mockups: Brand identity exploration with legible company names, taglines, and typographic arrangements
  • Social media graphics: Quote cards, announcement graphics, infographic concepts with text overlay
  • Book cover concepts: Title typography combined with illustrative imagery in a single generation
  • Signage and packaging: Product label concepts, storefront signage, and package design mockups

Ideogram vs DALL·E 3 for Text Rendering

DALL·E 3 made significant improvements to text rendering compared to earlier models, but Ideogram still leads by a significant margin. In head-to-head tests, Ideogram achieves accurate text rendering in approximately 90% of generations, while DALL·E 3 achieves approximately 70%, and Midjourney/Stable Diffusion rarely exceed 30–40% for complex text.

The gap is most apparent for: long strings of text (more than 2–3 words), decorative or script fonts, text with special characters, and layouts with multiple separate text elements. For these cases, Ideogram is the clear choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Ideogram different from other AI generators?

Ideogram's defining capability is accurate text rendering in generated images. While most AI models produce garbled text, Ideogram was trained to render readable, well-styled typography — making it the top choice for poster design, logo concepts, and graphic design work.

Can Ideogram generate images with readable text?

Yes. This is Ideogram's core differentiator. Specify text in quotes within your prompt (e.g., "a vintage poster with the text 'SUMMER SALE' in bold serif font"). Ideogram renders the text accurately with proper letterforms, spacing, and styling in the vast majority of generations.

How are Ideogram prompts formatted?

Ideogram uses natural language descriptions. Text to be rendered in the image should be placed in quotes within the prompt. You can also specify typography style (serif, sans-serif, handwritten, bold), color, placement, and font personality.

Is Ideogram free?

Ideogram offers a free tier with 10 slow generations per day. Paid plans start from $7/month for faster generation and more daily images. This prompt generator is completely free.
