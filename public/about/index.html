About ImageToPrompt

Making AI image and video generation accessible to everyone.

The Mission

ImageToPrompt exists to make AI image and video generation accessible to everyone. We believe the barrier to creating stunning AI art shouldn't be knowing how to write a perfect prompt. You have the vision — we handle the vocabulary.

The Problem We Solve

Every AI model speaks a different language. A great Midjourney prompt fails in Stable Diffusion. DALL-E 3 wants natural sentences while Flux wants camera specifications. Kling wants cinematic motion descriptions while Runway wants duration-specific framing. Writing model-specific prompts from scratch takes time and expertise that most creators don't have.

ImageToPrompt solves this by analyzing any image or text description and generating a professional prompt formatted specifically for your chosen model — complete with the right syntax, parameters, and vocabulary. What used to take 20 minutes of research and trial-and-error now takes 3 seconds.

The Technology

ImageToPrompt is powered by Claude AI Vision from Anthropic — one of the most capable multimodal AI models available. Our analysis examines composition, lighting, color palette, style, mood, and technical details to generate prompts that capture the essence of any visual.

The platform is built with React and TypeScript, deployed on Vercel's global edge network for fast response times worldwide. Rate limiting is handled through Upstash Redis to keep the tool fair and free for everyone.
