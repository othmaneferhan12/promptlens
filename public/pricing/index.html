Frequently Asked Questions

Is ImageToPrompt really free?

Yes, completely free. You get 10 AI prompt analyses per day at no cost, with no credit card or login required. The limit resets every day at midnight.

Will there be a paid plan?

A Pro plan is in development. It will offer higher daily limits, priority processing, batch image analysis, and API access. Sign up above to be notified when it launches — early subscribers will receive a founding member discount.

What's included in the free plan?

Everything: all 7 image models (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, Flux, DALL-E 3, Adobe Firefly, Leonardo AI, Ideogram), all 8 video models (Veo, Kling, Runway, Pika, Luma, Sora, Minimax, Stable Video), all 6 prompt styles, creative remix prompts, negative prompts, prompt history, and multi-language support. No hidden limits, no watermarks.

Why is there a 10/day limit?

Each analysis uses Claude AI's vision model, which has a real cost. The 10/day limit lets us keep the tool completely free for everyone. If you regularly need more than 10 analyses per day, the Pro plan will be for you.
